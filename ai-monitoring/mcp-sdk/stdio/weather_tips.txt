General Weather Safety Tips:

1.  Stay informed: Monitor local weather forecasts and alerts from reliable sources.
2.  Prepare an emergency kit: Include water, non-perishable food, a flashlight, batteries, and a first-aid kit.
3.  Know your risks: Understand the types of weather hazards common in your area.
4.  Have a family communication plan.
5.  If thunder roars, go indoors! Lightning is a serious threat.
6.  Never drive through flooded roads. Turn around, don't drown.
